How to use this web app

1) Open the formulary section .

2) Fill the customer information that is ask, and scroll down.

3) Fill the address information them click on validate, now you will see the address that you described in entered address and in certified address an address that exists and is the closest we can find , if you see this fields writen in green it means you address is certified to be correct, if not please check.

4) Close the formulary section by clicking on the edges of the window.

5) Now you will see all the information you entered formed below the ship to title, all text must be green, red means there is a error.

6) by clicking on copy information you will be able to copy all data in order to your clipboard.
